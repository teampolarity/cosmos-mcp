Cosmos · browser history

Ships meaningful pages from your local browser history into your cosmos graph. Filtering runs in this extension; only the pages that look like real content reach the server.

Sign in at cosmos.polarity-lab.com/connectors, click + add in the MCP clients section, name it something like browser-extension, copy the pmk_… string.
URL · title · hostname · last-visit timestamp · visit count · the literal string "web" as source. We never send page bodies, cookies, or form data. Everything else is filtered out client-side before the POST.
Navigational noise (inbox, feed, root pages of social hubs), your own deployed sites, OAuth and search-result URLs, untitled tabs. The full list lives in shared/filter-rules.json and is shared with the cosmos-mcp CLI so the desktop sync and the extension agree on what's signal.
